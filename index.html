Other Notes


The O'Reilly study reported a detection of a gender gap of $13K against women. The model intentionally does NOT include that term as we do not want to enforce an explicit bias. One might also want to replace Age with some measure of experience as we would not really want to use Age explicitly. For example replace "Age" with 25 + Years of relevant work experience.
